Well-Being

Rest & Relaxation

Taking some time out to relax is an important way of helping minds and bodies switch off from daily pressures. To help your child to relax, you could:

  • help them to take the time to pause and take a break
  • encourage them to take notice of the world around them
  • inspire creativity and imagination through art, writing, and reading
  • encourage them to practice yoga or meditation for kids

Sleep

Getting enough sleep can enhance mood and promote feelings of energy and well-being. To encourage your child to sleep better:

  • try to make your child more active throughout the day
  • encourage your child to do the same things in the same order before bed can help
  • make sure their electronic devices are switched off at least an hour before bed
  • make sure monitors, screens and devices are switched off before bed, as light from devices can interfere with sleep
  • eat dinner at least 2-3 hours before bedtime

Self-Esteem

Poor self-esteem can be damaging on general mood, health and well-being. To encourage good self-esteem:

  • encourage your child to focus on their strengths and positive thinking
  • support them to recognise the skills that they have
  • encourage them not to dwell on the things they might not be so good at
  • set small, realistic goals when doing something new, and try and reach them
